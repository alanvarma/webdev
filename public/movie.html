The Best Movies/shows According to Alan

My top 3 movies/shows of all-time.

Spider-man: Across the Spider-verse

Great story-telling, amazing artwork, beautiful music and just phemominal work putting it all together.

Demon slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

amazing story brought to television. Adaptation is on-par and just does great work at showing each characters growth. makes you love the villians also.

White Collar

Great character development throughout the show, never did i feel the show was weak or the story didnt make sense. Everything was done just right.
